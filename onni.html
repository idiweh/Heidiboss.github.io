Onni ja onnillinen mies

Opetuspeli koiran hankintaan ja hoitoon.
(Translates to "Onni and a man with onni - educational game about taking care of a dog")

The purpose of the thesis project was to produce an educational game that provides information about owning and caring for a dog. The game is a 2D mobile game for Android devices and was produced for Pelinpala Oy. The target audience for the game is primarily elementary school-aged children in families considering getting a dog.

I developed the project solo, based very loosely on a demo Pelinpala Oy provided, so everything (excluding the graphics of the dog character) was drawn and coded by me. The game is produced with Unity and the graphics are drawn in Photoshop.

The thesis can be read in the Theseus collection.

In the game the player moves the dog character Onni with an on-screen joystick. There are 10 levels, and from each level Onni must collect five scrolls that have information about dog care based on the theme of the level. The info-texts are collected in a library, so the player can read them again. When all five are collected, the player advances to the next level.

Onni can get hurt by things like snakes, ticks and cars etc, that are dangerous for dogs in real world also. The dangers vary based on the time of the year, for example, there are no snakes in the autumn and no poisonous mushrooms in the summer.
