Pocket Wow

3D World of Warcraft inspired mobile game for Android, made in a month as an university innovation project with Juho Halttunen (@Finn).

This game started as a huge list of features we wanted to practise using on an mobile environment. After a while we listed the most important ones and borrowed the concept of "starter area boars" from World of Warcraft, where you start the game by iconically killing low level wild boars.


The "trailer" shows off quite well the features we considered most important:

- Taking, tracking and completing a quest
- Inventory and collecting items
- Selling and buying items
- Equipping and switching equipments and weapons
- Character advancement: different classes and skills

In this game I also wanted to hone my coding skills in addition to modeling and animating all the characters of the game. I also practised character animation and rigging.

The game is made with Unity and in a mont we managed to make a working Demo of the game. The demo includes the beginning of the questline, some basic equipments, shop properties and couple different enemies in different environments. The behavior of the enemies is controlled by a state machine. Environment props like trees and bushes are from an asset pack.
