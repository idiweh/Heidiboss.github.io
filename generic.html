About me

Physiotherapist / Engineering student / Entrepreneur

Hello!

I am 30 years old soon graduating ICT-engineering student in Metropolia UAS, currently working on my thesis and majoring in game development. I have graduated 2015 as a physiotherapist from Lahti University of Applied Sciences and worked as a physiotherapist in public healthcare for 6 years.

I am currently working as an entrepreneur with my own trade name CBS 3D-studio. As the name suggests, CBS 3D-studio offers 3D-modeling solutions for games and commercial use. For the last year I have made 3D furnitures, photorealistic product images and interior renders for several major furniture brands in Finland. You can find examples in here.

My long term goal is to combine my two professions in making games and applications to enhance the patients rehabilitation. I believe games make rehabilitation more motivating, spontanious and efficent. If You are intrested in my first official healthcare themed game project, you can check out the X-ray Game I made with a classmate for radiology students.

After the official part I can list you some of my intrests and hobbies. On my free time I always have some kind of project going on, wether it's a videogame project (playing or making), painting commission or fixing a bike or a computer. Currently my hobbies include bouldering, biking and hiking. I also have three small dogs.

I love to photograph and since the COVID lockdowns I have had a lot of time to walk in the woods and photograph birds. You can check out my wildlife photography in here: @hl_pov
