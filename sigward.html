Sigward

Sigward is a completed 2D turn based strategy game made as an university project in a team of six. Everything in the game is made from scratch and my role included graphics and coding the graphics.
Sigward can be downloaded from Itch.io.

"The year is 3210 after the great explosion on earth. Human population has set its base called The New World on the planet of Bromer. The leader of the colony is Sigward, a commander of newly founded security troops, who is set on a mission to secure the surroundings. When commander Sigward goes missing during his operation YOU will have to gather troops to find him... before it's too late."

In the game the player has a map of the planet with some already discovered locations, like different established villages and found enemy lairs. But one must be aware, because the danger lurks on the path as well. From cities the player can purchase equipments and recruit new troops.

The battle is turn and grid based and the game has various different environments to battle in.

The troops are fully customizable with different stats based on armor and weapons, and the enemies abilities vary depending on the alien type.
